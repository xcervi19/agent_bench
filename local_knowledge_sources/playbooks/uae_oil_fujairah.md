# UAE oil / Fujairah

**Commodity:** crude | products  
**Geography:** UAE — Ruwais / Jebel Ali / Fujairah (outside Hormuz)  
**Last reviewed:** 2026-07-23  

## Executive summary

ADNOC sets Murban and UAE grade policy; Fujairah is the strategic East-of-Suez storage and bunker hub outside Hormuz. Scan must catch first: adnoc.ae (Murban/OSP/ops), moei.gov.ae policy, AD Ports / Fujairah port status, and ICE Endex Murban pricing — plus Hormuz risk that raises Fujairah’s bypass value.

## Price drivers (trader lens)

1. ADNOC Murban OSP and production adjustments.
2. Fujairah inventory builds/draws and port restrictions.
3. Hormuz risk premium boosting outside-strait logistics.
4. ICE Endex Murban vs Dubai/Brent structure.
5. OPEC+ UAE compliance vs capacity expansion narrative.

## Infrastructure & logistics

Ruwais and other Gulf load points sit inside Hormuz risk; Fujairah on the Gulf of Oman is the classic outside-Hormuz storage/bunker node. AD Ports links UAE maritime logistics. See fujairah_storage_hub and oil_benchmarks_and_spreads.

## Geopolitical triggers

- Hormuz escalation elevating Fujairah stocks/freight.
- ADNOC force majeure or Murban quality/ops event.
- Fujairah port or tank farm incident.
- OPEC+ UAE quota surprise.
- Regional diplomatic break affecting Gulf insurance.
