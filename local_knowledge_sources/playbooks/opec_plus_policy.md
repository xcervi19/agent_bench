# OPEC+ policy

**Commodity:** crude  
**Geography:** OPEC+ producers (MENA, Russia, selected others)  
**Last reviewed:** 2026-07-23  

## Executive summary

OPEC+ sets the marginal barrel via quotas, compensation cuts, and voluntary adjustments led by Saudi Arabia and Russia. Scan must catch first: OPEC.org communiqués and MOMR compliance tables, Saudi ministry/Aramco OSP signals, Russian energy ministry / Rosneft production cues, and major member force majeure that changes effective supply vs quota.

## Price drivers (trader lens)

1. Announced OPEC+ production targets vs observed compliance.
2. Saudi unilateral voluntary cut / boost and OSP differential policy.
3. Russian export volumes under sanctions constraints (effective, not quota).
4. Member disruptions (Libya, Nigeria, Iraq) creating involuntary underproduction.
5. IEA / EIA independent production estimates contradicting OPEC narrative.

## Infrastructure & logistics

Quota barrels move mainly via Middle East Gulf loadings (Ras Tanura, Fujairah/Ruwais complex, Kuwait ports), Russian seaborne / pipeline routes, and African Atlantic/Mediterranean load ports. Effective supply ≠ quota when ports, pipelines, or sanctions bind — cross-check member and chokepoint playbooks.

## Geopolitical triggers

- Emergency JMMC / ministerial meeting called with short notice.
- Saudi voluntary cut change or OSP shock to Asia/Europe differentials.
- Russia “pledge” vs actual seaborne loadings under price-cap/sanctions.
- Libya NOC force majeure or Iraq southern export interruption.
- Compensation schedule for past overproduction announced or missed.

## Tier 2 context sources

IEF statements; national MFA talking points from producer capitals (mofa.gov.sa, mofa.gov.ae, etc.) for diplomatic framing — not production truth. Secondary to MOMR + independent agency checks.

## Anti-patterns

- Anonymous “delegate said” social posts without OPEC.org confirmation.
- Treating nameplate capacity as available supply.
- Ignoring involuntary outages when reading compliance tables.
- Using only secondary media for quota math.
